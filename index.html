Research

My research interests include network control and optimization. In particular, my projects are related to decentralized data sharing and risk-averse optimization.

Control and Optimization

Perturbation is common in control systems and it can be due to out-of-distribution (OoD) time series, natural noise, adversarial perturbations, or differential privacy mechanisms. My projects discuss how perturbations in the parameters of the optimization problem can affect the control system or the optimization results.
